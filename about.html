About

An active, but thoughtful automotive software engineer with good communication and analytical skills looking to develop a career as an associate within an international business environment. From 2013 to 2022, he was an automotive softeware engineer specializing in Advanced Driver Assistance Systems at the Vehicle Solutions company of LG Electronics. During the period, he contributed to the development and production of lane detection algorithms for Mercedes Benz mono cameras for NCAP 2020+. Since 2022, he has been a senior software engineer with the HiL/SiL systems at DXC Luxoft company. His research interests include software architecture, design pattern, vision-based automation and sensor fusion system.
